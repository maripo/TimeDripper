TimeDripper

Thank you for installing.

We recommend pinning the TimeDripper icon to your toolbar.

Click on the extension icon in the toolbar to display the list of extensions. Find TimeDripper, and select "Pin to toolbar" from the gear icon menu on the right.

Having the icon visible allows you to:

  • Roughly understand the remaining time.
  • Know if the current site is consuming your time quota.
    ON OFF
  • If you check “Display remaining time on badge” in settings, the remaining time will be displayed on the badge.

Clicking on the icon allows you to edit the settings.

  • Sites
    • Block list Sites that are subject to time limits. You can use ‘*’ as a wildcard.
    • Allow list Sites that are not subject to time limits. This list takes precedence over the Block list.
  • Settings
    • Max Quota The maximum time limit. Viewing time will not recover beyond this time.
    • Recovery Interval The interval at which time limits recover.
    • Recovery Amount The amount of time that recovers.

For example,

  • Max Quota = 10 minutes
  • Recovery Interval = 6 minutes
  • Recovery Amount = 1 minute

With these settings, your time limit recovers by 1 minute every 6 minutes. After an hour from being empty, the full 10 minutes will be recharged. Try different settings to match your lifestyle and rhythm.
